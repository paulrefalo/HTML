Famous Haikus

Issa

The wren
Earns his living
Noiselessly.

Soseki

Over the wintry
forest, winds howl in rage
with no leaves to blow.

Issa

Winter seclusion -
Listening, that evening,
To the rain in the mountain.

Basho

An old silent pond...
A frog jumps into the pond,
splash! Silence again.

Kato Shuson

I kill an ant
and realize my three children
have been watching.
