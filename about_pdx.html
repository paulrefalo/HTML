Portland History

  • On February 8, 1851, the city was incorporated
  • Was known simply as "The Clearing" in the 1840s
  • Was also called "Stumptown" by outsiders
  • In 1912 drinking fountains known as "Benson Bubblers" were installed

Portland Facts

  • Portland was named by the flip of a coin. If it had landed on the other side, Portland would be known as Boston, Oregon.
  • Portland is the only major US city to have a dormant volcano within city limits.
